The Juggernaut

June 7, 2025

“Twenty days… my calendar says”

But time is already folding in,
because with each breath I take,
you are already coming—
rolling closer
on wheels carved by centuries
and faith eternal.

The drums will beat like the pulse of the earth,
ropes pulled by hands calloused with love,
the sky itself will bow
as you leave your sanctum
to meet your people—
no curtain, no threshold, no caste,
just devotion laid bare
like the open palms that pull your rath.

Twenty days.
And still,
every moment
is already yours!
